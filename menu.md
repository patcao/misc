## Cold Smoke Ideas

Treat cold smoking as a flavor step, especially in an apartment.

- Smoke Caesar components instead of raw fish:
  - Mayo or oil for dressing
  - Rye breadcrumbs
  - Parmesan
  - Salt
- Other smoke-friendly items:
  - Butter
  - Blue cheese
  - Tomatoes
  - Cooked eggs
  - Nuts or seeds

## Dessert Notes

Sweet corn panna cotta fits the menu because it is make-ahead, seasonal, and a little unexpected.

Blueberry sauce direction:

- Blueberries
- Lime zest and juice
- Sugar
- Pinch of salt
- Freshly ground black pepper, about 1/8 to 1/4 teaspoon for 8-10 servings
- Optional bay leaf while simmering

The black pepper should not read as "pepper sauce"; it should make the blueberries taste deeper, more floral, and less flat.

## Prep Timeline Sketch

### 3-4 Days Ahead

- Pickle or prep any garnishes
- Plan agua fresca flavors and syrups
- Shop for meat, fish, dairy, and produce

### 2 Days Ahead

- Braise short ribs, chill, and defat
- Make panna cotta
- Make blueberry sauce
- Smoke or prep Caesar components

### 1 Day Ahead

- Make mac and cheese components
- Prep Caesar dressing
- Toast watermelon seeds or breadcrumbs
- Wash and prep greens

### Day Of

- Reheat short ribs
- Bake or reheat mac and cheese
- Toss Caesar salad
- Finish agua frescas
- Sauce panna cotta before serving
