# Miso Black Cod

This is a potential fish main inspired by Nobu's famous black cod with miso. It is rich, sweet-savory, and make-ahead friendly because the fish marinates for 2-3 days before a fast broil or roast.

## Recipe Leads

### Nobu Black Cod with Miso

- Source: Glamour, adapted from Nobu
- Link: https://www.glamour.com/story/aaron-eckhart-date-recipe

This version uses black cod fillets marinated in a cooked mixture of sake, mirin, white miso, and sugar for 2-3 days. The fish is wiped, broiled until browned, then finished in the oven.

Unique/useful: The timing lines up perfectly with the current 3-4 day prep window. It gives a restaurant-y fish option without needing outdoor cooking.

### Easy Broiled Miso-Marinated Black Cod

- Source: Serious Eats
- Link: https://www.seriouseats.com/miso-cod-love-letter-11749925

This Serious Eats discussion points to a simpler home version of miso-marinated black cod and notes that black cod is actually sablefish, prized for its rich, buttery texture. It can be marinated from 30 minutes to 2 days depending on schedule.

Unique/useful: Helpful if you want a less ceremonial, more weeknight-friendly version. It also notes salmon as a more accessible substitute.

### Nobu-Style Miso Black Cod Reference

- Source: Food & Wine
- Link: https://www.foodandwine.com/trader-joes-miso-black-cod-11896843

This article discusses Nobu-style miso black cod and the core restaurant logic: sablefish marinated with miso, sake, and sugar, then cooked until caramelized.

Unique/useful: Good reminder that the key is fatty sablefish plus a sweet-salty miso cure. Lean cod will not behave the same way.

## Menu Notes

- This would be delicious, but the menu already has smoked whitefish Caesar and short ribs. Add it only if you want a second elegant protein or if the short ribs become optional.
- It pairs well with cucumber-mint agua fresca, pickled watermelon rind, and rice or simple greens.
- For 8-10 adults, consider smaller portions: 3-4 ounces per person if served alongside short ribs and mac.

## Working Direction

- Use sablefish/black cod if possible.
- Marinade: sake, mirin, white miso, sugar.
- Marinate 2-3 days for the Nobu-style version.
- Wipe off excess marinade before cooking so it caramelizes rather than burns.
- Broil or roast hot until browned and just cooked through.
