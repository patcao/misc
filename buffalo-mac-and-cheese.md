# Buffalo Mac and Cheese

This is the nostalgic comfort-food anchor. For the larger menu, the best version should be creamy, tangy, spicy, and cut with celery/blue cheese/herb crunch so it does not become a single heavy note.

## Recipe Leads

### Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese

- Source: Southern Living
- Link: https://www.southernliving.com/recipes/buffalo-chicken-mac-and-cheese

This is a practical baked 13-by-9-style version with rotisserie chicken, hot sauce, celery, onion, sharp cheddar, and a panko-blue cheese-parsley topping.

Unique/useful: Probably the best baseline for the party. It serves about 8, uses rotisserie chicken, bakes cleanly, and has the Buffalo wing garnish logic built into the topping.

### Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese

- Source: Allrecipes
- Link: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/245764/buffalo-chicken-mac-and-cheese/

This is a flexible stovetop-style version with rotisserie chicken, cheddar, Monterey Jack, hot sauce, and blue cheese or gorgonzola.

Unique/useful: Good for ratios and crowd expectations. It can be moved into a baking dish and finished with buttered panko, crushed crackers, or ranch-seasoned breadcrumbs.

### Mac and Cheese with Buffalo Chicken

- Source: Bon Appetit / David Burke
- Link: https://www.bonappetit.com/recipe/mac-and-cheese-with-buffalo-chicken

This is the showier version: cornflake-crusted fried chicken pieces folded into cheddar-provolone mac, then baked with hot sauce and butter.

Unique/useful: Use this if the mac is meant to be a major centerpiece. The cornflake chicken is a great texture idea even if you do not follow the full fried-chicken process.

### One-Pot Buffalo Chicken Pasta

- Source: Bon Appetit / Jesse Szewczyk
- Link: https://www.bonappetit.com/recipe/one-pot-buffalo-chicken-pasta

This is not a baked mac, but it uses chicken, Monterey Jack, blue cheese, hot sauce, and a cornstarch-stabilized cheese sauce in one pot.

Unique/useful: Helpful if oven space is tight or the day is hot. The stabilized sauce technique could make a smoother, less roux-heavy Buffalo mac.

## Menu Notes

- Best party direction: baked Southern Living-style base, but with extra garnish.
- Garnish ideas: celery leaf gremolata, pickled celery, scallions, blue cheese, ranch-panko crumbs, crushed cornflakes, or crushed potato chips.
- Consider serving the hottest Buffalo sauce on the side so the main pan stays crowd-friendly.
