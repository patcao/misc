An Apartment Supper for Eight to Ten

July Fourth

Smoke, cola, corn, watermelon, and bright pickles, arranged with a little ceremony.

To Begin

Smoked Whitefish Caesar

Crisp romaine, smoked whitefish, rye breadcrumbs, fried capers, sharp lemon dressing, and Parmesan.

Optional everything-spiced watermelon seeds

Compressed Watermelon

Vacuum-sealed watermelon cubes with seasoned juice, pickled rind, mint, scallion, lime, and chile.

Toasted seed crunch

Main Table

Coca-Cola Braised Short Ribs

Slow-braised beef with cola, tamarind, soy, ginger, garlic, black pepper, and a lime-bright finish.

Chilled overnight, defatted, and reheated gently

Buffalo Mac and Cheese

Baked macaroni, hot sauce, cheddar, blue cheese, celery leaf gremolata, scallions, and crisp crumbs.

Pickled celery on the side

Pitchers

Agua Frescas

Hibiscus ginger, pineapple jalapeno, and cucumber mint, chilled without ice and poured over fresh cubes.

Lightly sweet, high acid, very cold

Dessert

Sweet Corn Panna Cotta

Silky corn custard with blueberry-lime sauce, a whisper of black pepper, and optional bay.

Cornmeal shortbread or sugared watermelon seeds

Potential Addition

Miso Black Cod

Sablefish marinated with sake, mirin, white miso, and sugar, then broiled until lacquered and caramelized.

Nobu-style, 2-3 day cure

Vaguely patriotic. Firmly delicious.
